Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Encouraging local startups and technology adoption boosts economic growth and creates job opportunities for youth.

Sustainability

Promoting sustainable agriculture and renewable energy protects natural resources and mitigates climate change effects on vulnerable communities.

Infrastructure

Strengthening infrastructure through transparent governance enhances public services and attracts foreign investment, crucial for national development.

Empowerment

Empowering women and youth enhances workforce participation, driving innovation and productivity essential for Bangladesh's competitiveness in the global economy.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"I strongly believe that we can create a poverty-free world, if we want to.... In that kind of world, [the] only place you can see poverty is in the museum. When school children will be on a tour of the poverty museum, they will be horrified to see the misery and indignity of human beings. They will blame their forefathers for tolerating this inhuman condition to continue in a massive way."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
